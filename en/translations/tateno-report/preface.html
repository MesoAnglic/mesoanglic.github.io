Preface

The Aerological Observatory of Tateno was inaugurated on 26 August 1920. However, owing to a lack of money and personnel, the installations and the observations were not complete during the first few years. Now we are able to publish this first number of the report. In this number we give only the aerological observations by pilot balloon. The observations by kite, captive balloon and others will be published in the following numbers. Besides this report we have the "Bulletin of the Aerological Observatory of Tateno", which is issued three times each month to make the results of our observations known more quickly, though in less detail. A few other reports entirely in Japanese are also published.

October 1926.

The Director
of the Aerological Observatory of Tateno,
W. Oishi.
